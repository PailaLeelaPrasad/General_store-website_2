Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are the accepted payment methods?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and also offer cash-on-delivery (COD) option.

2. Is there a minimum order value for home delivery?

Yes, we have a minimum order value of $20 for home delivery. Orders below this amount can be picked up from our store.

3. Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we have a hassle-free return and exchange policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange the product within 14 days of delivery.

4. Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only offer shipping within [Your Country]. We do not provide international shipping options.

5. How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to check the status of your delivery.

6. Do you have a loyalty program for regular customers?

Yes, we value our regular customers, and we have a loyalty program that offers exclusive rewards, discounts, and special offers. Sign up at our store to join the loyalty program.
